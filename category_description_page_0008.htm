Speciality paper labels

Labels
Printer
White opaque (for blockout) paper labels - Matt & Gloss. Has a higher level of white pigment to provide an enhanced level of opacity. Ideal for when you need to block out printing or text underneath the label.
Laser & laser photocopier only for both gloss and matt. Inkjet will work on matt.
White FR8 for fridge and freezer use. These labels have an adhesive that is designed to stick to goods that are frozen already. Just wipe of the frost and stick the label to the frozen product. Matt & gloss.
Laser & laser photocopier only. Matt FR8 can be printed with an inkjet, but if the label gets wet the ink will run.
White WLK 202 for wine bottle labels. Matt and gloss. Designed by the wine industry specifically to go on to wine bottles. Enhanced adhesive and water resitance, so they can ber dunked in and ice bucket without disintegrating.
Laser & laser photocopier only. Matt WLK 202 can be printed with an inkjet, but if the label gets wet the ink will run. This is significant especially for white wines which are often cooled in an ice bucket.
Gold Foil - Matt & Gloss. Metallic foil labels that are ideal for presentation certificates and other decorative presentations.

Offset printing only.
 Laser & photocopier may work but the printing can scratch off easily. Not recommended or guaranteed.

Silver Foil - Matt & Gloss. Metallic foil labels that are ideal for presentation certificates and other decorative presentations.

Offset printing only.
 Laser & photocopier may work but the printing can scratch off easily. Not recommended or guaranteed.

Uncoated Ivory Wine Labels - Matt only. Ivory colour with a textured finish. Classy look on your wine bottles!
Laser & laser photocopier only. Uncoated Ivory can be printed with an inkjet, but if the label gets wet the ink will run. This is significant especially for white wines which are often cooled in an ice bucket.
Car Tyre Stock - Matt only. Car Tyre Stock has a very aggressive adhesive. Should be used only wher you need a label material that needs to stay stuck permanently. Once it is properly applied it won't come off.
Offset printing only.
 Laser & photocopier may work but the printing can scratch off easily. Not recommended or guaranteed.

Synthetic Labels

Labels
Printer

Polylaser gloss clear. The ideal clear label for a wide variety of uses. Because of its clear nature it is almost invisible when applied to a surface, so that your clients will see only the printing on the label.
Laser & photocopier only
Dataflex 303 PE White. This is the only synthetic label we stock that you can use with an inkjet printer - and it weatherproof too! The ink doesn't run, because the softer porous surface of the label absorbs the ink. Because of its very stretchy nature it is only available in a very limited number of shapes.
Inkjet OK. Also suitable for offset printing.
